Noah's Kindness Hub

Welcome to Noah's Kindness Hub, a student created space that keeps our friend Noah's spirit alive through everyday acts of caring and inclusion. Here you'll find short tribute videos, artwork, and a live feed of good deeds. All created by classmates who believe one small kindness can grow into something big. Scan, explore, and add your own spark of kindness to keep the circle going.
