Sharing Guidelines

  • Be specific: Describe the act of kindness in detail
  • Be respectful: Use appropriate language and content
  • Protect privacy: Use first names only
  • Add media: Include photos when appropriate (and with permission)
  • Add location: Note where the act took place (classroom, playground, etc.)
