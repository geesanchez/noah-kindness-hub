About Noah

Noah Reyes was a sixth-grader whose warm smile and genuine kindness touched the lives of everyone around him. His natural ability to make others feel welcome and valued created a positive atmosphere wherever he went. Though his time with us was brief, Noah's spirit of compassion and his commitment to making others feel included continues to inspire our school community.

This Kindness Hub was created to honor Noah's memory and continue his legacy of spreading joy and kindness. Through sharing stories of compassion and encouraging acts of kindness, we hope to keep his spirit alive and create a more caring world.

"The smallest act of kindness is worth more than the grandest intention."
