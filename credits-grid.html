Project Timeline

Week 1
Project kickoff: introduced the project, showed a QR demo, led a kindness brainstorming session, drafted the sitemap, and discussed potential pages for the site. No teams were formed at this stage.
Week 2
Content creation began through student classwork. Prompts were given to facilitate the creation of each page. Initial designs were reviewed and refined based on feedback.
Week 3
Final content integration and testing. QR code plaque was designed and prepared for installation in the school hallway.
Launch Day
Noah's Kindness Hub officially launched with a small ceremony. QR code plaque was installed and the first acts of kindness were shared.
