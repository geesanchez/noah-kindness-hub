Submit Your Work

We welcome submissions to our Kindness Gallery from students, teachers, and community members.

  • Photos: Share images of kindness in action or moments that remind you of Noah's spirit
  • Artwork: Submit drawings, paintings, digital art, or other creative expressions of kindness
  • Kindness Moments: Document acts of kindness you've witnessed or performed
  • Testimonials: Share how Noah or this project has inspired you

All submissions will be reviewed before being added to the gallery. Please include your first name and grade level with your submission.
